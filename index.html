About Us

Tomorrows Lifesavers is dedicated to making first aid training accessible, engaging, and effective. We believe that everyone should have the skills to help in an emergency—because the right response in the first few minutes can make all the difference.

Our courses are delivered by experienced, qualified instructors who bring real-world expertise to every session. We use hands-on, practical teaching methods that build genuine confidence.

Who We Work With

  • Schools and educational institutions
  • Workplaces and corporate teams
  • Community groups and organisations
  • Families and individuals
  • Sports clubs and youth groups

Our Services

We offer a range of courses tailored to different needs and skill levels.

❤️

CPR Training

Learn the essential techniques of cardiopulmonary resuscitation, including adult, child, and infant CPR. Includes AED training.

🩹

First Aid Essentials

A comprehensive introduction to first aid covering wounds, burns, fractures, choking, and medical emergencies.

👶

Paediatric First Aid

Specialised training for those working with or caring for children, meeting Ofsted and childcare requirements.

🏭

Workplace First Aid

HSE-compliant courses for designated workplace first aiders. Available as 1-day emergency or 3-day full courses.

🧠

Mental Health First Aid

Recognise the signs of mental health issues and learn how to provide initial support and guide someone to appropriate help.

🔄

Refresher Courses

Keep your skills sharp and certifications current with our refresher training sessions.
